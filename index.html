Workshop Resources

The 2026 DSU Singer/Songwriter class has been composing a song called Lost In Time and our task today is to create several album cover concepts for this new song.

Below you'll find the text that must be included on the cover, the full lyrics for your reference, some starter images you may want to use, and some links to other places where you may find other resources.

Key images from lyrics

  • Water or waves, especially turbulent water
  • Haze or murkiness (poor visibility)
  • Sand (possibly an hourglass, though this may be too obvious)
  • Crumbling or unstable structures
